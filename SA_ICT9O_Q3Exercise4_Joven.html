Boracay



All You Need To Know About boracay!


Boracay is a small island in the central Philippines. It's mostly known for it being a famous tourist spot. On Boracay, you would find beautiful beaches, sturdy palm trees, comfy resorts and many more. A famous spot in Boracay is the White Beach.


Must Tries In Boracay!


As someone who's been to Boracay, a recommended must try is the banana boat! The banana boat is a fun activity and in my opinion was one of the best things I did at Boracay. Though, Caution! The banana boat is quite fast and it could throw you in the salty waters of the beach. But I feel like being thrown in the water is part of the fun and is what some tourists want to happen.

Another must try is island hopping! Island hopping is when you travel from one island to another. This is especially common in places that has small islands near it. An example is Boracay! Here in the picture, you can see me and my family at Puka beach which we used a boat to get there.

Call to Action!


In conclusion, hop on down to Boracay and indulge in it's beautiful beaches and pristine waters. Participate in activities like island hopping, banana boat, jet skiing and along with your family. And all in all, just have fun!
